About Me

I am a machine learning leader and applied researcher/engineer with a decade of experience at LinkedIn, Apple, and Amazon, now serving as a Principal Machine Learning Engineer at Nubank.

I excel at creating and setting the technical direction for high-performing foundational AI teams for LLMs and RecSys, which in turn creates large revenue and engagement impact. I have a strong track record of publications at top conferences, having published the highest number of papers at LinkedIn from 2022 to 2025.

I have published over 25 papers at premier venues including ICML, ICLR, KDD, NeurIPS, CIKM, and WWW.

  • Recommendation Systems (RecSys)
  • LLM Efficiency
